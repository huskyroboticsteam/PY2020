\subsubsection*{template$<$int state\+Dim, int size, int param\+Size$>$\newline
class Noise\+Cov\+Mat$<$ state\+Dim, size, param\+Size $>$}

Represents a square noise covariance matrix. Returning the zero matrix can sometimes be dangerous depending on your model, as it may cause numerical instability or incorrect computations.


\begin{DoxyTemplParams}{Template Parameters}
